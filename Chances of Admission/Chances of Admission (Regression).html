Prediction of Admission¶

Description of Dataset¶

The dataset contains several parameters which are considered important during the application for Masters Programs.

List of Attributes¶

GRE Scores </b>( out of 340 )
TOEFL Scores </b>( out of 120 )
University Rating </b>( out of 5 )
Statement of Purpose </b>( out of 5 )
Letter of Recommendation Strength </b>( out of 5 )
Undergraduate GPA </b>( out of 10 )
Research Experience </b>( either 0 or 1 )
Chance of Admit </b>( ranging from 0 to 1 )

Objective¶

The objective of this project is to perform data cleansing, and required transformations and build a predictive model which predicts about their chances of admission for a particular university.
